"True happiness comes only by making others happy" -David O. McKay

I am a proud Texan, born and raised in El Paso. The oldest of four girls, I'm accustomed to high levels of responsibility. I have a creative streak and love to engage with others.

I'm passionate about music and in addition to 10 years of piano, I am familiar with the organ and love to sing. I taught piano to student ranging from age 5-13. This experience enhanced my organizational and communication skills.

I played on the Varsity Tennis Team throughout high school and learned the importance of hard work and diligence. As captain I learned how create an atmosphere of inclusion and encouraged team spirit.

Running, reading, and traveling are favorite hobbies of mine. I love to be outdoors and more than anything I love to be with people.
